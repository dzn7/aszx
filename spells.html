The Magic Spells of Crystalis


Refresh

MP Cost: MP lost over time as long as you are casting.

Obtained: Taught by Zebu, after opening the Sealed Cave in Wind Valley.

Description: Restores Life for as long as you channel the spell.

Telepathy

MP Cost: 8

Obtained: Taught by Tornel, after beating Stom in a duel.

Description: Cast to speak with the four Wise Men. Also gives you the ability to communicate with animals.

Teleport

MP Cost: 20

Obtained: Taught by Tornel after getting the Tornado Bracelet.

Description: Lets you teleport to any town or village you've visited.

Paralysis

MP Cost: 4

Obtained: Given by the imprisoned elder on Mt. Sabre.

Description: Puts enemies and NPCs to sleep for a short time.

Recover

MP Cost: 24

Obtained: Taught by Asina during your first meeting.

Description: Removes poison, paralysis, or petrification.

Barrier

MP Cost: MP lost over time as long as you are casting.

Obtained: Taught by Asina after stopping the whirlpools in the Angry Sea.

Description: Protects you from enemy projectiles, but not contact, for as long as you channel the spell.

Change

MP Cost: 20

Obtained: Taught by Kensu in return for the Love Pendant.

Description: Gives you the ability to disguise yourself in one of four forms.

Flight

MP Cost: MP lost over time as long as you are casting.

Obtained: Taught by Kensu after returning him to his normal form.

Description: Gives you the ability to fly over dangerous areas and enemies for as long as you channel the spell.
