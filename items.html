Consumable (Single-Use) Items


Medical Herb

Available in Shops: Leaf, Brynmaer, Oak, Nadare's, Portoa, Joel, Swan, Shyron, Goa

Description: Restores physical strength little by little; can be used while you fight. Restores 32 Life.

Antidote

Available in Shops: Leaf, Brynmaer, Oak, Nadare's, Joel, Swan, Shyron, Goa, Sahara

Description: Eliminates poison recieved from enemy attacks and traps.

Lysis Plant

Available in Shops: Portoa, Amazones, Goa

Description: Removes paralysis status condition.

Fruit of Lime Tree

Available in Shops: Portoa, Shyron

Description: Removes petrified status condition.

Fruit of Power

Available in Shops: Oak, Nadare's, Amazones, Joel, Swan

Description: Restores 1/4 of total MP; can be used during fights.

Fruit of Repun

Available in Shops: Sahara

Description: Removes enemy curse which changes you into a little monster.

Magic Ring

Available in Shops: Shyron, Sahara

Description: Restores entire MP; can be used during fights.

Warp Boots

Available in Shops: Leaf, Brynmaer, Nadare's, Portoa, Amazones, Swan, Goa, Sahara

Description: Teleports you back to a town or village you have visited.

Opal Statue

Available in Shops: None

Description: If you die, it replenishes all of your life power, and you can continue playing from the point where you died.

Alarm Flute

Available in Shops: Leaf

Description: Used to wake sleeping people.

Key Event Items


Windmill Key

Obtained: Given to you by the Windmill guard.

Description: Needed to start the windmill in Wind Valley.

Insect Flute

Obtained: Given to you by the elder of Oak.

Description: When played in the right place, the Insect Monster appears.

Prison Key

Obtained: Given to you by the imprisoned Mayor of Leaf.

Description: Unlocks the prison door in the ice maze.

Key of Styx

Obtained: Given to you by Zebu, in Shyron.

Description: Unlocks the gate atop Mt. Hydra.

Fog Lamp

Obtained: Found in a cave east of Portoa.

Description: Needed to board the ship from Portoa to the Angry Sea.

Shell Flute

Obtained: Given to you after healing a dolphin with a Medical Herb.

Description: Calls dolphins to help you cross the sea.

X-Ray Goggles

Obtained: Given by Clark in Zombie Town.

Description: Lets you see through the secret storage room door.

Broken Statue

Obtained: Defeat Sabera.

Description: Becomes the Gold Statue when you use the Glowing Lamp.

Glowing Lamp

Obtained: Given by Kensu in the Joel windmill.

Description: Changes the Broken Statue into the Gold Statue.

Gold Statue

Obtained: Use the Glowing Lamp on the Broken Statue.

Description: Stops whirlpools in the Angry Sea so you can cross it.

Love Pendant

Obtained: Found in a cave behind the Queen of Portoa's chamber.

Description: Give it to a wise man, and he will teach you the magic of disguise.

Kirisa Plant

Obtained: In a cave southeast of Portoa.

Description: Give it to the Chief of the Amazones tribe, and she will give you a bow and arrows.

Ivory Statue

Obtained: Defeat Karmine.

Description: Used to restore a wise man to his normal form.

Bow of the Moon

Obtained: Given to you by Aryllis, the Amazones Queen.

Description: Destroys the Statue of the Moon.

Bow of the Sun

Obtained: Found atop Mt. Hydra.

Description: Destroys the Statue of the Sun.

Bow of Truth

Obtained: Given to you by Azteca.

Description: Use it to destroy the Emperor of Draygonia.

Flute of Lime Tree

Obtained: Found in the waterfall cave, or given to you by the Queen of Portoa.

Description: Restores petrified people to normal.

Statue of Onyx

Obtained: Found in a patch of grass across the river from Brynmaer.

Description: Give it to Akahana in the town of travelers, and he will give you the Gas Mask.
