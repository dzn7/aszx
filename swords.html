The Swords of Crystalis


Sword of Wind

The Sword of Wind was left in the capable hands of the elder of Leaf Village to be given to the hero magician when he awakes from his long slumber.

Obtained: Given by the elder of Leaf Village.

Special Effect: Hero can destroy rock walls at level 2.

Power-ups: Ball of Wind (Level 2), Tornado Bracelet (Level 3)

Sword of Fire

Long revered by the Dwarves of Oak Village, the Sword of Fire remains in their constant care. Perhaps the hero magician can convince them to part with it for the good of the world.

Obtained: Given by the elder of Oak Village.

Special Effect: Hero can destroy ice walls at level 2.

Power-ups: Ball of Fire (Level 2), Flame Bracelet (Level 3)

Sword of Water

The Sword of Water waits for the hero magician deep within a cave below Mt. Sabre hidden behind a waterfall. The cool, pure water of the mountain gives the sword its incredible power.

Obtained: Found in the Waterfall Cave near Portoa.

Special Effect: Hero can form ice bridges over water at level 2.

Power-ups: Ball of Water (Level 2), Blizzard Bracelet (Level 3)

Sword of Thunder

Too powerful to be left in the hands of humans, the Sword of Thunder lies deep within the the caves of Mt. Hydra, waiting to be retrived by the hero magician.

Obtained: Found in a chest in Styx.

Special Effect: Hero can destroy iron walls at level 2.

Power-ups: Ball of Thunder (Level 2), Storm Bracelet (Level 3)

Crystalis

The ultimate sword, Crystalis, can only be forged by using the mechanical power of the floating tower to combine the four elemental swords.

Obtained: Created by combining the four elemental swords on the floating tower.

Special Effect: Fires a powerful blast of energy at level 1.
