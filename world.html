The World of Crystalis

My personal interpretation of the Crystalis world map (click for full image).

Leaf

Armor Shop

  • Carapace Shield ($80)
  • Tanned Hide ($100)

Item Shop

  • Medical Herb ($30)
  • Antidote ($40)
  • Warp Boots ($60)
  • Alarm Flute ($50)
The town where it all begins. You wake up emerging from a strange machine just outside the small village of Leaf. Several villagers have been expecting your arrival and will provide you with some much needed resources, including the Sword of Wind!

Brynmaer

Armor Shop

  • Carapace Shield ($70)
  • Bronze Shield ($220)
  • Leather Armor ($140)

Item Shop

  • Medical Herb ($35)
  • Antidote ($45)
  • Warp Boots ($65)
After reparing the Windmill and making your way through the cave it reveals, you find yourself near Brynmaer, the town of travelers. Compared to quiet Leaf, Brynmaer is quite the busy town. Be sure to check out the new equipment available at the Armor Shop. Also, there's a man named Akahana who seems to have lost something important to him...

Oak

Item Shop

  • Medical Herb ($50)
  • Antidote ($60)
  • Fruit of Power ($80)
The town of Oak is home to the Dwarves, protectors of the powerful Sword of Fire.

Nadare's Inn

Item Shop

  • Medical Herb ($60)

  • Fruit of Power ($100)
This small inn at the foot of Mt. Sabre (NE) is the perfect place to rest and relax after a long day of skiing or before a long day of taking the fight to General Kelbesque and his lackeys.
