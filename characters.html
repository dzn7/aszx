The Frozen Magicians


The Hero

You play the unnamed protagonist: a mighty magician cryogenically frozen during The Great War. A hundred years later you've been awakened with your memories, even your name, lost to time. The Four Sages task you with finding the four elemental swords, defeating Emperor Draygon and destroying his terrible floating fortress.

Mesia

Another magician from the past frozen during the Great War. She awakens before you and awaits your arrival. She will guide you on your journey to defeat Emperor Draygon.

The Sages


Zebu

Waits near Leaf Village for news of the hero magician's awakening.

Tornel

Lives with his pupils in a training school north of Brynmaer.

Asina

A great healer who waits for the hero near the Captial of Water, Portoa.

Kensu

Preoccupied with matters of a... personal nature, this master of disguise is always on the move.

Azteca

The great leader of the four sages.

The Draygonian Empire

Emperor Draygon

Leader of the powerful Draygonian Empire. He seeks to reactivate the floating tower.

General Kelbesque

The Draygonian general who imprisoned and enslaved the people of Wind Valley at the summit of Mt. Sabre.

Sabera

The deceptive Draygonian sorceress who has taken over the island west of Joel and turned its inhabitants into zombies.

Mado

A powerul assassin sent by the Emperor to eliminate threats to the Emperor's reign.

Karmine

The finest of the Emperor's warriors.

Miscellaneous

Akahana

A refugee from the town of Goa. He claims to have lost a rare and unusual statue.

Stom

A warrior training at Tornel's training school.

Ralph

Mayor and lighthouse keeper of Joel Village. (Trivia: Ralph and his brother are the main characters in SNK classic "Ikari Warriors".)

Clark

Ralph's brother who's gone to investigate the Evil Spirit Island and not yet returned...

Aryllis

Leader of the people of the town of Amazones.

Rage

Protector of the sacred Lime Tree, which houses Mesia's cryogenic chamber.

Deo

One of Stom's favorite bunnies.
